One afternoon,
a room learns music.

Richmond Park Library, Bangalore — 24 May 2026. Jason Zachariah brought a keyboard, seven swaras, and twenty curious people. Two hours of rhythm, melody, and harmony later, the room was singing three-part choir on Toto's Africa. These are the photographs.
